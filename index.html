SAPA Technologies Ltd. is a electronics manufacturing company located in Burnaby, BC, where I had the privilege of working as a Technician. At SAPA Technologies, the focus is on small scale OEM, with a special emphasis on Surface-Mount Technology (SMT).

Surface-Mount Technology, or SMT, is a pivotal aspect of modern electronics manufacturing. It involves mounting electronic components directly onto the surface of printed circuit boards (PCBs), as opposed to the traditional method of inserting components through holes in the board. This innovative approach offers several advantages, including a reduction in component size, improved manufacturing speed, and enhanced overall efficiency.

During my time at SAPA Technologies, I actively engaged with SMT processes, playing a crucial role in verifying product arrivals, managing component intake, and ensuring that the assembly process of flexible LED light sheets operated smoothly. This included the task of soldering through-hole components onto PCBs, using software testing equipment to maintain the quality and consistency of LED light sheets
